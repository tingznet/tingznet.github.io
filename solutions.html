Digital Worksite

Worksite oriented processes and easy to manage sensor network can help you maximise your profitability of your machine park.

Connected Machines

Developed and validated in the construction site.

Tingz creates a practical sensor network for construction sites, mines and other widespread industrial work zones, making it possible to communicate with all construction equipment on site.

Tingz platform and sensor network is developed and tested in construction sites in partnership with one of the biggest construction companies in Turkey. Innovative telematics and capable software platform also enables companies to integrate different IoT solutions and 3rd party applications.


Collect Data From All Machines & Equipments

Tingz sensors can be mounted on every machine & equipment on your worksite. Specially configured sensors can identify whether machine is idle running, working or stopped.

Tingz platform can easily work with other sensors, IoT applications and different data sources.

Reduce Idling

Spot underutilised equipments and long idling times with instant alerts or periodic reporting.

Maximize Your Profitability

Monitor your productivity on site by tracking activities in different workzones, number of trips and long waiting spots.

Track Your Fleet

Monitor your fleet activity, distance traveled, fuel consumption, location and total working hours.
